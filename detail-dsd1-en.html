// Deutsches Sprachdiplom der Kultusministerkonferenz · Lycée Théodore Deck, Guebwiller

The DSD1 (Deutsches Sprachdiplom der Kultusministerkonferenz) is an official German language diploma awarded by the Standing Conference of the Ministers of Education and Cultural Affairs (KMK) of Germany. Internationally recognised, it certifies an intermediate level of German through 4 tests covering all language skills. Obtained at the Lycée Théodore Deck in Guebwiller on 09 June 2023.

What this diploma validates

📖 Reading comprehension (A2)

  • Reading everyday texts in German
  • Understanding simple messages
  • Identifying main information

🎧 Listening comprehension (B1)

  • Understanding dialogues and announcements
  • Following conversations on everyday topics
  • Extracting essential information

✍️ Written expression (B1)

  • Writing simple, coherent texts
  • Describing experiences and events
  • Arguing on familiar topics

🗣️ Oral expression (B1)

  • Communication on everyday topics
  • Structured oral presentation
  • Interaction in concrete situations

Context

🇩🇪 Alsace — bilingual French-German region

Living in Fessenheim, a few kilometres from the German border, I developed my German in a naturally bilingual context. The DSD1 officially formalises this intermediate level. My high school curriculum included enhanced German teaching as part of the Franco-German programme.
