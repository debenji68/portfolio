SAÉ 1.01 — BUT R&T IUT Colmar 2025–2026

IT Security and Cybersecurity Awareness

3 minute video + slideshow · Cybersecurity · Communication · Teamwork

The goal of SAÉ 1.01 was to produce a 3-minute video and a slideshow on a cybersecurity topic. My team worked on password security and digital hygiene best practices: how to choose a strong password, understand brute-force attacks and apply ANSSI recommendations.

Project description

🎬 Video (3 minutes)

  • Topic: password security
  • Accessible to a non-expert audience
  • Demonstration of a brute-force attack
  • Application of Moore's law to attacks
  • ANSSI recommendations and best practices

📊 Slideshow

  • Structured presentation of the topic
  • Statistics on common passwords
  • Illustration of concrete risks
  • Applied practical advice
  • Verified and referenced sources

👥 Teamwork

  • Collaboration in a group of 3 students
  • Distribution of roles and tasks
  • Coordination and cross-reviews
  • Oral presentation to the group
  • Meeting imposed deadlines

Tech stack

Cybersecurity Oral presentation Slideshow Video editing Communication ANSSI Passwords Digital hygiene

What I learned

  • In-depth study of password security principles and brute-force attacks
  • Explaining a technical topic to a non-specialist audience
  • Collaborative work: coordination, task sharing, meeting deadlines
  • Oral communication and presenting to a group
  • Using official resources (ANSSI, CNIL) for information research
