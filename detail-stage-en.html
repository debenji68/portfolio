9th Grade Observation Internship Jan. 2022

Crédit Mutuel — Fessenheim branch

Banking sector · Professional discovery · 1 week

A one-week observation internship as part of 9th grade at the Collège Félix Éboué. This immersion at the Crédit Mutuel branch in Fessenheim allowed me to discover the professional banking world: from customer reception to file management, through information systems. This first experience strengthened my curiosity for IT tools in a business environment.
